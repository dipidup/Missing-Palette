Hey! I am Ankit. An engineer by profession and an artist by heart. I completed my formal education from BIT Mesra, Ranchi as a Civil Engineer. Currently I am based in Pune, India. Here I work for an MNC as a Decision Analytics Associate in a Business Consulting space. My passion for art developed during the school days and it turned into my 'Go to Place'. During my engineering days I was fortunate enough to collaborate with various artists and art groups which helped me strengthen my love for colors. During th initial days of my Engineering I started conducting art workshops in and around the town, and till date I have conducted more than 15 art workshops.
Art has always been my 'Go to Place' and whenever I get time I love to spend a part of it with a canvas and colors. Although I love working with all the mediums but watercolor is something which I love handeling the most. I always keep a travel sketchbook along with a set of watercolors whenever I travel or visit any place which is worth painting.
I have a few exhibitions and art projects under my hood and I love spending my free time on various commisioned work as well. I do take orders for customised art works and realistic portraits. Looking forward to hearing from you!
